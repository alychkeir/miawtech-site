The smart cat flap that recognises each animal by RFID, grants or denies access, and keeps you informed in real time – all without ever sending your data over the internet. Control it from your smartphone via local Bluetooth, schedule its times and receive notifications directly on your phone.

Features designed for you

RFID identification

Each cat gets a unique badge. Only authorised animals can enter or leave. Easy learning mode.

Remote control

Open or close the cat flap from the app via Bluetooth (range ~10 m). No server, no latency.

Scheduling

Set daily automatic opening hours. Schedules are stored locally on the device.

Real‑time notifications

Get a notification as soon as your cat enters or leaves. Notifications are local – never sent over the internet.

Activity tracking

View passage history (graphs, export CSV). All data stays on your phone.

Privacy by design

No account, no cloud, no data collection. Your privacy is protected by design.

How it works ?

1

Installation

Mount the flap on your door, plug in the power. The device does not need internet.

2

Register cats

In the app (Bluetooth), give each cat a name and photo. Activate learning mode to pair its RFID badge.

3

Customise

Set opening hours, enable notifications. All settings are stored on the flap.

4

Enjoy

Your cat can move freely while you keep full control via Bluetooth, without relying on an external server.

Data protection

A 100% offline cat flap

MiawTech is designed to respect your privacy. No internet connection is needed for operation:

  • No Wi‑Fi: Communication is exclusively via local Bluetooth between your smartphone and the flap.
  • No account: You don't need to create any account or provide an email address.
  • No personal data collected: No information (cat names, history, settings) is transmitted outside your device.
  • Local notifications: Alerts are generated directly by the app on your phone, without passing through a server.
  • Data stored locally: History and profiles remain on your phone; you can export them as CSV if you wish.

In accordance with GDPR, we do not process any personal data. Your privacy is protected by default.
